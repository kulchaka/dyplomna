For Customers

This site is the graduation project of a Yandex.Practicum student. Its purpose is to show what the student has learned, what technologies he possesses.

The layout of the site is based on the BEM methodology. This makes the site flexible and scalable. In addition, adding new pages and functionality is easier and faster than in projects made by other methodologies. The site is also easier and cheaper to maintain.

News cards come from the server and are processed asynchronously. That is, the site continues to work as long as the server responds. This increases the ease of use of the service.

When creating the project was used to build technology Webpack. This is a modern approach to site development. The code is optimized, which means the site runs faster. Also, the site works in older browsers. Even if visitors are users of older operating systems, they won't have display problems. This means that the potential audience increases.

The project has been tested by Yandex.Practicum experts. If your business needs a website, we recommend contacting this student.
